Marching Band

The Marching Knights take to the field in the Fall performing at SFS Football games, home and away! The Marching Knights keep the fans engaged throughout the game. At halftime, Mr. Collier and his team arrange for a memorable show every week playing classic hits all season long.


Pep Band

The pep band plays at every home basketball game. From the start of the season to the end, the SFS Pep Band helps keep the crowd engaged and provides energy.


Concert Band

The concert band plays more traditional music in a concert setting. They hold two concerts a year: one in December and one in Spring. The concert band is sure to always draw a crowd!



Band Camp

Every year, our marching band spends a week at Camp Nuhop, in Ashland County, Ohio. We spend the week learning our halftime show for that season. It isn't all work though: there's lots of oppertunities for activities and bonding, and kids don't take themselves very seriously.


The band director

The band has been under the direction of Mr. Carl Collier for almost 40 years.

Beyond the Band Room Doors

Our band students don't just do band. We provide them the time and flexibility to continue doing the other things they enjoy. From musical to art to horseback riding, you don't have to give up what you already do to join band!


Want to join band?

If you want to join band, you can fill out the interest form. This isn't a commitment; you can decide later not to. If the form doesn't load, try disabling your ad blocker.


Not sure yet?
